Week 1 — Foundation (Days 1–7)
Post 1 TikTok/day. 3 X posts/day. 3 LinkedIn posts. Fill out all bios with keyword-rich descriptions. Follow 50 accounts in your niche. Reply to 10 posts/day. Goal: 50–200 followers total.
Week 2 — Content Rhythm (Days 8–14)
Identify your top performing post from week 1. Make 3 variations of it. Start repurposing TikToks as IG Reels. Post 1 YouTube Short. Engage with trending hashtags. Goal: 200–500 followers total.
Week 3 — Double Down (Days 15–21)
Double posting frequency on your best platform. Collaborate with 1 other creator (quote tweets, duets). Post a thread on X covering Ghana + US news. Start newsletter signups. Goal: 500–1,000 followers total.
Week 4 — Push for 1K (Days 22–30)

🔑 The #1 Rule
Consistency beats quality at the start. Post every day even when it feels pointless. The algorithm rewards creators who show up daily. Your 10th post will be better than your 1st. Just ship it.

🖼️ YouTube Thumbnail & Title Ideas

🎨 Proven thumbnail formulas that get clicks:
Shocked face + bold text
Your reaction face + "YOU WON'T BELIEVE THIS" text. Highest CTR formula.
Before/After split
Two images side by side. Works great for transformation and comparison content.
Number + promise
"5 things nobody tells you about..." Numbers in thumbnails = more clicks.
Arrow pointing at you
Point at bold text. Draws eye. Works for opinion and reaction content.
